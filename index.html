Meet the Architect

If we're going to spend some time working together, it's only fair that you get to know the guy behind the website. My name is Austin, and I earned my Bachelor of Computer Science (BCS) in 2016 from a small-town Massachusetts college. My passion for computer science stretches all the way back to my middle school days, when I was making Sonic the Hedgehog "fanpages" in Microsoft Frontpage. Fortunately I moved past Frontpage, and during my studies I took a particular interest in making beautiful, accessible applications and websites. I focus on a simplistic, "less-is-more" approach to my designs. I believe that the days of cluttered websites, drenched in often unecessary information are far behind us. Anyone, young or old, should be able to navigate the internet with ease, and I want to help make that possible. I look forward to working with you!
